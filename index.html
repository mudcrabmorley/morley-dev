Aus MuayThai


Client Background

Aus MuayThai is a news and events company dedicated to promoting Muay Thai across Australia. With a strong social media following, they approached me to help expand their brand by developing a website to provide their audience with the latest information.


Pain Points

Although the company had success sharing content on social media, they wanted to offer more in-depth coverage. They also needed a platform to host a voting system for their annual Muay Thai awards, allowing users to cast votes in each category.


Solution

We built the website using WordPress with the Kadence page builder, complemented by plugins to post events and display news articles. Additional plugins were installed to monitor and optimise SEO, and to embed YouTube feeds showcasing popular fight replays. A custom-coded Jotform voting system was also integrated and styled to match the website’s theme.


Result

The website now receives around 300 monthly visitors, providing up-to-date news and information to Muay Thai fans across Australia. The annual voting system received over 1,500 votes and will be used for future awards. The site's functionality and design continue to evolve with iterative improvements.


Key Technologies Used

  • WordPress
  • Kadence theme and block editor
  • Custom CSS
  • Jotform
  • Google Site Kit & SEO Framework


J Squared Constructions


Client Background

J Squared Constructions is an expert carpentry company based on the Gold Coast. As their business expanded, they approached me to develop a website to enhance their branding and online presence.


Pain Points

Although the client had some content ideas and a rough vision for their brand, they were uncertain about how to fully develop it and provide customers with a clear understanding of their services and values. They also wanted to connect their brand to the digital world but were unsure about the necessary technology.


Solution

We started by designing the site’s landing page in Figma, creating custom icons to represent services and selecting images to showcase recent construction projects. Once the design was approved, the website was built using WordPress with the Spectra page builder, along with custom CSS and JavaScript for additional styling and animation. The site was optimised for fast loading times, and a Google Workspace account was set up to integrate a custom domain and email, as per the client’s request.


Result

The client now has a strong online presence to promote their business and secure contracts, supported by an informative and user-friendly website. The use of WordPress allows for easy updates and future marketing enhancements if J Squared chooses to focus more on SEO and lead generation.


Key Technologies Used

  • Figma
  • WordPress
  • Spectra page builder
  • Custom CSS & JavaScript
  • Google Workspace


NTG Fight & Fitness


Client Background

NTG Fight & Fitness is one of the most popular MMA gyms in Southern Brisbane. They approached me to modernise their website and find a more cost-effective hosting solution than their current setup.


Pain Points

The existing website was not responsive across different device screen sizes and lacked a cohesive style guide. They also wanted to integrate their Shopify store for selling fight gear to members.


Solution

We rebuilt the website using custom code with the Tailwind CSS framework. This allowed for consistent styling and efficient adaptation to different screen sizes. In addition to a custom contact form and Shopify store integration, custom icons were designed to visually represent the various fighting classes offered on the welcome page.


Result

The new website offers improved accessibility and has modernised the brand’s online presence. The site now attracts daily visitors and continues to generate over 35 monthly enquiries via form submissions, contributing to the growth of the gym’s community. Custom coding the site has also significantly reduced hosting costs while ensuring the site remains lightweight and fast.


Key Technologies Used

  • Custom code (HTML, CSS)
  • Tailwind CSS
  • Shopify
  • DALL·E 3 AI
  • GitHub
